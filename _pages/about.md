I am a Junior Research Group Leader at the [Institute of Biostatistics and Informatics in Medicine and Ageing Research](https://ibima.med.uni-rostock.de/) at the University Medical Center Rostock, Germany.

My research applies computational and statistical methods to address fundamental questions in biology and medicine, with emphasis on:

* High-throughput -Omic molecular data analysis
* Microbiome and Metagenomic data analyses and interventions
* Microbial genomics and bioinformatics
* Computational drug repositioning
* Aging Research and Longevity
